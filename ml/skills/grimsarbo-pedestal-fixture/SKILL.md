# GRIMSARBO pedestal fixture

This is a source-manual benchmark fixture for a small round pedestal table with red base and wood top.

## Visual identity

- Round wood tabletop with center hole.
- Red painted rim, cone apron, cylindrical column, and four-arm cross base.
- Black leveling feet under each arm.
- Dimension target: 40 cm diameter, 43 cm high, 11 cm top/cone depth.

## Source assembly sequence

1. Install leveling foot `195312` into each of four arm blocks.
2. Slide the fourth arm horizontally into the central hub/column assembly.
3. Tighten four `10074126` screws with Allen key `100092` at the hub.
4. Fasten top mounting plate with three `115461` screws.
5. Drop/align top disc onto center mount.
6. Press tabletop down.
7. Check level and adjust four feet.

Do not substitute this with a wedge-tab DIY design when the requested mode is source-manual replica.
