# Manual page layout

Use sparse black line art on white pages. The page should be readable without prose.

## Layout rules

1. Large step number near the top-left of each step region.
2. Parts needed appear near the step number: isolated part drawing plus `Nx`.
3. Main assembly drawing occupies the largest region.
4. Use solid black motion arrows for insert, drop, push, rotate, and flip actions.
5. Use circular zoom insets for small hardware interfaces.
6. Use rectangular inset panels for top-down alignment or hidden underside details.
7. Put footer page/code metadata outside the instructional drawing.

## Multi-step pages

When two small steps share a page, stack them vertically:

- Step 1: inventory/part prep on top.
- Step 2: larger assembly operation below.

Do not fill pages with explanatory paragraphs.
