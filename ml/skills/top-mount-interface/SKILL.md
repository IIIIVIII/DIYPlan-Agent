# Top mount interface

Round pedestal tables often connect the top to the column through a center plate, hole, or twist-lock disc.

## Rules

- Preserve the center hole on the tabletop if visible.
- Preserve underside mounting plate count and screw count.
- Show hidden column or plate with dashed lines in an inset.
- Use `drop_vertical` for placing a disc or tabletop onto the center mount.
- Use alignment marks, not prose, when the source uses tabs/notches/finger icons.

## Required details

- Top disc
- Center hole
- Mounting plate
- Screw count and SKU
- Hidden underside target
